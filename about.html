About KVS

Connecting the KNUST Community.

Project Overview

The KNUST VoIP System (KVS) is the official telecommunication infrastructure for the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology. Designed to facilitate seamless internal communication, KVS connects academic colleges, administrative units, halls of residence, and service centers through a unified IP-based network.

Who We Serve

Our network spans the entire campus ecosystem, providing reliable voice services to:

  • Colleges: Engineering, Science, Health Sciences, Art & Built Environment, Humanities & Social Sciences, Agriculture.
  • Administration: Central Admin Blocks A & B, Finance, Procurement.
  • Student Services: DOSA, Counselling Center, Hospital.
  • Residential: All Halls of Residence and Staff Quarters.
  • Emergency Services Police Station, Electricity Bulk House, Security Unit, Fire Service.
